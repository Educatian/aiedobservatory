Welcome to Educator Licensing

The Utah State Board of Education (USBE) issues educator licenses to qualify individuals to work in Utah’s public schools.  This site provides general licensing information, procedures, and license records.

USIMS

The Utah Schools Information Management System (USIMS) access is available at https://usims.schools.utah.gov.

Public Educator Look-up Tool

Educator licenses are public records.  You may search for any educator, school, or district/charter to view any educator’s public record in the USBE Educator Licensing Look-up Tool Utah State Board of Education Educator Look-up Tool.  (School information is not required.) Please see Educator Qualifications for license requirements for educators.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) and Contact Info

We consistently update our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) to meet the needs of applicants and already licensed educators. The Educator Licensing team is available by phone or in-person.  Please schedule an appointment for a virtual or in-person appointment. 

To Schedule an Appointment:  Utah State Board of Education - Educator Licensing (Schedule an Appointment)
