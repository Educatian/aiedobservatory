The field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) will continue to grow at a rapid pace, with new abilities, new platforms, and new functions appearing frequently.  This website includes resources to support educators with the rapid evolvement of Generative AI in K-12 education settings. 

Announcements

ODE recently updated resources for the use of generative Artificial Intelligence (genAI) in Oregon schools (for a one-page summary of the latest pages, please see our summary document):
• Generative Artificial Intelligence in K-12 Classrooms offers general guidance for Oregon school and district leaders planning, implementing and evaluating genAI tools into their school culture. 
• Developing Policy and Protocols for the Use of Generative AI in K-12 Classrooms guides leaders through a process for developing guidance or policy related to the safe, ethical, equitable, and effective use of genAI.
