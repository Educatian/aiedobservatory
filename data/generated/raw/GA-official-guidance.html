Leveraging AI in the K-12 Setting

GaDOE’s guidance outlines general information and best practices to ensure the ethical, effective, and secure use of AI tools and systems within our schools. Through continued collaboration, training, and adherence to these guidelines, Georgia schools can harness the power of AI to create a more personalized, efficient, and innovative educational environment for all students.
