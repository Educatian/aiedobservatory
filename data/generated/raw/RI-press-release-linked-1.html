Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Rhode Island Schools

In August 2025, RIDE released released guidance to support local education agencies (LEAs) across the state in navigating and implementing policies and practices for the responsible, effective, and equitable use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in classrooms.

The goal of RIDE’s AI Guidance is to provide information and current perspectives and practices regarding using Artificial Intelligence in Rhode Island schools and to provide a structure for ongoing conversations. The distribution of this guidance doesn’t constitute formal recommendations or regulations. RIDE also released a video in which students, educators, and school leaders discussed the potential of AI and the newly released guidance.

Click here to view RIDE's Guidance for Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence in Schools

Using Google NotebookLM, an AI-powered study app, and with RIDE's Guidance for Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence in Schools as the source material, RIDE created a "Deep Dive" podcast episode during which two AI hosts discuss the Guidance. 

Click here to listen to RIDE's AI Guidance Podcast

 

RIDE Resources: These tools are specifically designed to use with RIDE's AI Guidance document.

  • Custom RIDE Guidance GPT

RIDE’s AI Guidance Series was a professional learning experience designed to help Rhode Island educators and leaders understand, govern, and thoughtfully integrate artificial intelligence across teaching, learning, and operations. 

Together, these sessions provide a shared foundation, practical guidance, and a student-centered vision for using AI to support future-ready learners and school communities. If you were unable to attend any of these sessions, recordings for all AI Guidance Sessions are linked below:

  • RIDE AI Guidance Session 1: AI Foundations & Vision 10.9.25
  • RIDE AI Guidance Session 2: Governance & Instruction 10.16.25
  • RIDE AI Guidance Session 3: Equity & Inclusion 10.23.25
  • RIDE AI Guidance Session 4: Future-Ready Students 10.30.25
  • RIDE AI Guidance Session 5: Legal & Security 11.6.25
  • RIDE AI Guidance Session 6: Operations & Community 11.13.25
